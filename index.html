Fun Facts

Tim Berners-Lee and his First Web Page

The world's first web page was created by English scientist Tim Berners-Lee on August 6, 1991. It still exists and contains information on what the World Wide Web is, how to install a web server, how to download a browser, and more.

How Many People Use the Internet?

At the beginning of 2020, more than 4.5 billion people used the Internet. The total population of the Earth was estimated at 7.8 billion, meaning a significant part of humanity had not yet connected to the Internet.
